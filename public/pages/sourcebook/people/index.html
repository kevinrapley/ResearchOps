PEOP-COMM

People

The People pillar covers research roles, capability, community practice, mentoring, wellbeing, collaboration and professional standards.

Operating question

What roles, skills, communities and support are needed for responsible research practice?

Strong people systems protect research quality while making research easier to learn, support and sustain.

Glossary

Capability
The practical ability to plan, run, support, review or use research at a level appropriate to risk and context.
Community of practice
A group that helps people share research standards, learning, examples and support across teams.
Supervision
Named support and oversight for people doing research work that is unfamiliar, higher risk or beyond their current independent competence.
Researcher wellbeing
The conditions, support and workload controls that help people doing research make careful judgements without avoidable harm.
