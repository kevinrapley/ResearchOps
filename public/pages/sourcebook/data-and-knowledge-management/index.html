DATA-STO-ACC

Data and knowledge management

The Data and knowledge management pillar covers evidence capture, repositories, taxonomies, metadata, data retention, access control and knowledge reuse.

Operating question

How is research evidence stored, protected, retrieved, reused and connected to decisions?

Research knowledge compounds when evidence remains findable, trustworthy, governed and connected to decisions after individual projects end.

Glossary

Research repository
An approved system or structured collection used to store, describe, retrieve and reuse research evidence.
Evidence trace
The link between a research claim and the source material, method context, consent conditions and limitations that support it.
Retention rule
A documented rule that states what research material is retained, reviewed, archived or deleted and who approves exceptions.
Knowledge reuse
The controlled use of existing research evidence to inform a new decision, reduce duplicate work or frame future research.
