REC-ADMN

Recruitment and administration

The Recruitment and administration pillar governs the full participant operations system, including sourcing, screening, scheduling, consent, privacy, incentives, participant records, communications, reporting and learning.

Operating question

How are participants sourced, invited, consented, supported, paid, tracked and protected throughout the research lifecycle?

Recruitment operations should reduce delivery friction while protecting participants, improving sample quality and making the real cost and value of participant access visible.

Glossary

Support model
The decision about whether recruitment is self-service, full-service or hybrid.
Sample rationale
The documented reason a participant group, source and screening approach are appropriate for the research question.
Participant record
The operational record used to manage contact, consent, scheduling, incentive and participation status.
