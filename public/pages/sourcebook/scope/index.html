SCOPE

Scope

The Scope pillar defines the boundaries of research work, request intake, prioritisation, risk classification, method choice and support model.

Operating question

What research should happen, who may do it and what level of support or review does it need?

Clear scope directs limited research capacity towards the highest-value questions and stops low-quality or unsafe research from entering delivery unnoticed.

Glossary

Research scope
The agreed boundary of a research activity, including question, participants, method, intended use, evidence standard and support level.
Decision need
The decision, risk or uncertainty that research evidence is expected to inform.
Support level
The degree of ResearchOps, research leadership or governance involvement needed for a piece of work.
Self-service pattern
An approved low-risk approach that enables non-researchers or teams to collect limited evidence within defined guardrails.
