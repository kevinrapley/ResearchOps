ENVIRO

Environment

The Environment pillar covers research spaces, fieldwork conditions, remote research settings, accessibility, researcher safety and participant comfort.

Operating question

Where and under what conditions can research happen safely, inclusively and effectively?

Good environments reduce session risk, improve participant trust and make high-quality research repeatable across locations and channels.

Glossary

Research environment
The physical, remote, digital, fieldwork or community setting in which research is planned, run, observed or supported.
Environment adjustment
A change to the setting, channel, timing, support or tools that helps a participant or researcher take part safely and effectively.
Fieldwork setting
A research setting outside a controlled research space, including homes, workplaces, public places and community locations.
Environment issue
A material problem with access, safety, privacy, comfort, technology or setting suitability that affects research quality or care.
