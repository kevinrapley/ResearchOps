Organisational context

The Organisational context pillar connects research operations to strategy, funding, decision rights, portfolio planning, stakeholder management and operating constraints.

Operating question

How does research fit the organisation's strategy, governance, funding, decision-making and delivery rhythm?

ResearchOps creates value when operational systems are connected to the organisation's priorities and decisions.

Glossary

Operating model
The way research people, processes, governance, tools, funding and decision routes work together to create value.
Decision rights
The explicit ownership of who can make, approve, defer or escalate decisions informed by research.
Portfolio rhythm
The repeated planning and review cycle that connects research demand, evidence gaps and operating improvements across teams.
Operating value
The value ResearchOps creates by reducing risk, improving decision quality, protecting participants, saving effort and making evidence reusable.
