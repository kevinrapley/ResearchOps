INFRA-PROV

Tools and infrastructure

The Tools and infrastructure pillar covers research tooling, procurement, integration, support, access, automation, resilience and operational ownership.

Operating question

What tools, platforms, integrations and support processes make research reliable, secure and scalable?

Tools should reduce repeat effort, protect data and support the operating model instead of becoming unmanaged local workarounds.

Glossary

Tool owner
The person accountable for a research tool's approved use, access, support, assurance, lifecycle and escalation route.
Critical research tool
A tool whose failure or misuse could materially affect recruitment, consent, participant care, data protection, evidence quality or research delivery.
Integration record
The documented source, destination, data, owner, permissions, failure mode and audit route for a tool or workflow integration.
Lifecycle plan
The plan for adopting, reviewing, supporting, changing and retiring a research tool or infrastructure component.
