What every artefact must show

Provenance
Source project, study, method, date range and linked evidence.
Confidence
Evidence strength, sample coverage, limitations and known gaps.
Reuse guidance
Where this evidence can be reused and where it must not be applied.
Review state
Published, due review, stale or withdrawn.

Team decision for this page

Information architecture

Lead with findability. The page starts with search, then offers controlled routes by service area, user group, method and risk.

Service design

Make backstage governance visible. Users need to know why repository evidence is trustworthy before they reuse it.

Research Operations

Keep curation separate from creation. Only reviewed artefacts appear in the repository; candidates move through a queue.
