How to write retention duration

Use P followed by a number and a unit.

  • D for days, for example P30D
  • W for weeks, for example P8W
  • M for months, for example P12M
  • Y for years, for example P2Y

You can combine date units in order, for example P1Y6M . If you need hours, minutes or seconds, add T before the time part, for example PT2H or P1DT12H .
