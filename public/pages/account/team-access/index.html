Request access to a team

Ask to join a team before using research records that belong to that team.

A Team Admin must approve your request before you can use that team’s research records. This request will not give you a role or access to sensitive information.

What happens next

  • A Team Admin will review your request.
  • Your request will appear on your account page as “Awaiting approval”.
  • You cannot use this team’s research records until your request is approved.
