# ResearchOps privacy and data protection readiness

This folder records the privacy and data protection evidence needed before ResearchOps can support a SOC 2 or ISO/IEC 27001 claim.

It does not assert DPIA approval, ROPA completion, lawful-basis approval or GDPR compliance. It records the draft privacy evidence that needs Home Office service-owner, information-asset-owner, privacy, legal and security review.

## Evidence position

The privacy and data protection control is now partially evidenced because the main personal-data classes, flows, purposes, supplier touchpoints and review questions are named in version-controlled service documentation.

The control is not complete until:

- DPIA screening has been completed and approved through the Home Office route
- the ROPA entry has an owner, review date and approval reference
- lawful basis and any Article 9 condition have been confirmed for each purpose
- the data map has an owner, version date and review cadence
- data-sharing, processor, subprocessor and transfer positions have been agreed
- retention and deletion rules have been signed off for each data class
- participant information, privacy notices and consent wording align with the approved position
- service owner, information asset owner, privacy, legal and security roles have signed off the evidence

Until then, ResearchOps can describe this as privacy and data protection readiness evidence. It must not be described as an approved DPIA, completed ROPA, approved lawful-basis decision or completed GDPR assurance.
